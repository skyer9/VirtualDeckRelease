Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

VirtualDeck ("the App") is a Mac menu bar utility that lets you create a custom button grid for launching apps and sending keyboard shortcuts. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

VirtualDeck does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App has no server-side component and makes no network requests.

Data Stored Locally

The App stores your button configuration (app bundle IDs, key codes, icon choices, layout preferences) in a local file on your Mac using UserDefaults or the app's sandboxed container. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the Export Settings feature and share the resulting file yourself.

Permissions Used

  • Accessibility (접근성) — Required to send keyboard shortcut events to other applications. The App uses this only when you click a keystroke button.
  • Apple Events (앱 실행) — Required to launch other applications on your behalf when you click an app-launch button.
  • File Access (파일 접근) — Required only when you use the Export / Import Settings feature to read or write a .vdeck file you have selected.

None of the data accessed through these permissions is transmitted outside your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated date above will reflect the change. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
